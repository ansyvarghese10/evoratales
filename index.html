About Evora Tales

Evora Tales is an independent media collective exploring the intersections of people, nature, travel, and wildlife through narrative and visual storytelling. We create written narratives, print editions, and documentaries that highlight culture, environmental conservation, and sustainable development.

What We Offer

We offer storytelling and media services that align with our mission. Our work supports organizations, individuals, and eco-projects working on sustainability, conservation, and cultural documentation.

  • Documentary & short film production
  • Environmental writing & longform articles
  • Nature & wildlife photography
  • Photo essays & visual storytelling
  • Content creation for eco-projects & NGOs
  • Educational media & awareness campaigns
  • Storytelling workshops & collaborations
