About Evora Tales

Evora Tales is an independent media collective exploring the intersections of people, nature, travel, and wildlife through narrative and visual storytelling. We create written narratives, print editions, and documentaries that highlight culture, environmental conservation, and sustainable development.

Through both words and visuals, we tell grounded, immersive stories—capturing the beauty and complexity of the world through photography and film. Our work aims to inspire thoughtful travel, advocate for environmental stewardship, and reveal the deep connections between culture and the natural world.

We also collaborate on educational media, awareness campaigns, and storytelling workshops that promote sustainability and cultural understanding, connecting people with critical environmental and cultural narratives.

Our Services

We offer a range of services designed to bring your environmental and cultural stories to life:

  • Documentary and Short Film Production: Crafting compelling visual narratives.
  • Environmental Writing and Longform Articles: Engaging content that informs and inspires.
  • Nature and Wildlife Photography: Capturing the essence of the natural world.
  • Photo Essays and Visual Storytelling: Powerful narratives told through images.
  • Content Creation for Eco-Projects and NGOs: Tailored media solutions for impactful initiatives.
  • Educational Media: Developing resources that promote sustainability and cultural understanding.

About the Founder

Ans Y Varghese is a freelance photographer, writer, and traveler based in Kerala, India.

With a background in Political Science and a passion for nature, storytelling, and sustainable living, he created Evora Tales to share moments that inspire awareness, connection, and creativity.
